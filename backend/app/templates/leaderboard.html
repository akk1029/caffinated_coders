How CO₂ is calculated

Each ingredient you mark as Used counts as food saved from landfill. We estimate 0.25 kg per unit and apply the standard 2.5 kg CO₂ per kg of food waste factor (WRAP / IPCC methodology). Money saved is the estimated retail price of consumed items.
